About Me

Lindsey Kopacz is a self taught developer. She first started out teaching herself Drupal 7 in 2012, and quickly started catching onto sitebuilding and module development. She started her first development job in May 2014 and exponentially improved her skillset in Drupal.

Lindsey is now a front end developer who is passionate about inclusion and diversity in the technology field. She speaks about a variety of topics, but particularly about Accessibility. See below to see slides or speaking events.

Lindsey loves yoga, dancing, makeup artistry, and feminism. In her free time you can find her teaching herself JavaScript, learning vegan recipes, and trying fun craft beers!
